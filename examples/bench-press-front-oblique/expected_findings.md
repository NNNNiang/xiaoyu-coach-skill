# Expected Findings: Bench Press Front-Oblique

## 中文验收要点

这份示例用于测试小鱼教练对卧推单动作的评估能力。生成报告时不需要逐字复制本文件，但应保留下面这些判断。

### 本示例测试什么

- 单动作评估流程。
- 从脚端斜前方机位判断卧推的推举路径和控制。
- 杠铃推类动作的安全优先级表达。
- 能说明当前机位能看什么、不能看什么。

### 必须观察到

- 说明脚端斜前方机位适合看杠铃路径、手腕是否垂直承重、肘部外展是否明显、脚和下肢是否稳定、每次推起节奏是否变慢。
- 说明这个角度不适合高置信度判断触胸点、肩胛后缩、卧推反弓和杠铃下放深度。
- 检查手腕是否大体在杠铃下方，不能只看重量是否推起。
- 检查肘部是否明显过度外展，或在当前角度下基本可接受。
- 检查杠铃每次路径是否大体一致。
- 检查脚和下肢 setup 是否稳定。

### 安全优先级期望

- 报告必须包含安全优先级。
- 卧推安全应围绕肩部位置、手腕承重、杠铃控制、保护者或安全杠是否可见来表达。
- 如果视频无法确认保护或安全杠，应以教练语言说明：大重量卧推需要更清楚的保护/安全设置。

### 不应误判

- 不要从这个角度高置信度判断肩胛后缩质量。
- 看不到清晰触胸时，不要断言具体触胸点。
- 不要写成泛泛的卧推动作百科；每条判断都要回到当前视频。
- 不要暴露姿态引擎、抽帧、模型识别等内部术语。

### 拍摄建议期望

- 当前脚端斜前方机位适合快速测试。
- 更严格复核卧推时，应增加侧面或侧后方机位，用于观察下放深度、触胸点、胸廓/反弓和肩部位置。

## English Baseline

Use this file as a regression target when testing Xiaoyu Coach on this sample. The exact wording of a generated report may differ, but the report should preserve these coaching judgments.

## What This Sample Tests

- Single-exercise assessment flow.
- Bench press setup and pressing-path review from a front-oblique angle.
- Safety-priority language for a barbell pressing movement.
- Filming-angle feedback that explains what this angle can and cannot judge.

## Required Observations

- Mention that the front-oblique / foot-end angle is useful for seeing bar path, wrist stacking, elbow flare, foot stability, and pressing rhythm.
- Mention that this angle is limited for judging touch point, shoulder-blade position, bench arch, and exact bar depth.
- Check whether the wrists stay reasonably stacked under the bar.
- Check whether elbow flare is obviously excessive or within an acceptable range from this angle.
- Check whether the bar path stays reasonably consistent rep to rep.
- Check whether the feet and lower-body setup remain steady.

## Safety Priority Expectations

- The report should include a safety-priority section.
- Bench press safety should be framed around shoulder position, wrist stacking, bar control, and whether protection / rack safety is visible or needed.
- If the report cannot verify spotter or safety-bar setup from the video, it should say so in coach language and recommend safer filming/setup for heavier bench work.

## Do Not Misjudge

- Do not claim shoulder-blade retraction quality with high confidence from this angle.
- Do not claim exact bar touch point if the view does not show the chest contact clearly.
- Do not turn the report into a generic bench tutorial; tie every point back to what is visible in this video.
- Do not expose internal pose-engine or frame-selection terminology.

## Expected Filming Advice

- Keep this front-oblique angle for quick public testing.
- For stricter bench review, add a side or rear-side angle that shows bar depth, touch point, ribcage/arch, and shoulder position.
