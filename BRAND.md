# Brand and Identity Policy

This repository is open source, but the Xiaoyu Coach identity is not
given away with the source license.

## Reserved Identity

The following are reserved by the project owner unless explicit written
permission is granted:

- Xiaoyu Coach, 小鱼教练, 小yu教练, and localized variants.
- Xiao Yu Coach, xiaoyu-coach, and confusingly similar service names when
  used as a product or commercial coaching identity.
- Logos, avatars, mascot artwork, icon concepts, app icons, report visual
  identity, slogans, and brand presentation.
- Any use that suggests endorsement, official partnership, or that a
  modified service is the original Xiaoyu Coach service.

## Allowed References

You may use the name for truthful, non-confusing references, such as:

- "built with the Xiaoyu Coach Skill"
- "fork of xiaoyu-coach-skill"
- "compatible with the Xiaoyu Coach public report format"

## Not Allowed Without Permission

Do not use the reserved identity to:

- Launch a paid or free coaching service that appears to be official.
- Repackage the project under the Xiaoyu Coach name.
- Use Xiaoyu Coach logos, avatars, or mascot art in an app, website,
  marketplace listing, course, model, dataset, or commercial report.
- Claim that your fork, service, or model is endorsed by the original
  project owner.

## Forks and Modifications

Forks are welcome under the repository license. Modified versions should
use their own product name and visual identity, and should clearly state
that they are independent from the original Xiaoyu Coach project.

This policy is not a trademark registration notice. It is the repository's
brand-use rule and a reminder that Apache-2.0 does not grant trademark
or brand identity rights.
